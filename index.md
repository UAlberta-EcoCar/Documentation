Welcome to EcoCar ⚡  Electrical ⚡

This is the documention for the EcoCar Electrical team. For the official website see [ualberta-ecocar.ca](https://www.ualberta-ecocar.ca/).
New members should first become familiar with using Github.

Short Intro to the things we do here:

* PCB Design
* Power Electronics (Stuff not taught in any course at UofA) Ex: GaN and SiC based power converters.
* Complex Industrial and Automotive Embedded Systems; Programming for Reliability, Efficiency and Cost savings.
* Wire harness design; Incorporating Weight savings with cost and time to integrate
* Multi System Integrtation Ex. Fuel Cell control integrates with power electronics and Vehicle drive systems.
